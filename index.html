Our Causes

Empowering youth through education, growth, and skill-building across communities.

Bridging Rural-Urban Education Gaps

Many rural students still lack access to quality learning and equal academic opportunities. Shikkhar Alo focuses on removing these disparities through grassroots engagement. Our programs are built to empower the underserved youth of Bangladesh.

Source: Prothom Alo · Jan 10, 2023

Moving Beyond Rote Learning

Bangladesh’s education system heavily relies on memorization over real understanding. This hinders students' critical thinking and real-world application. Shikkhar Alo promotes skill-based learning through practical experiences.

Source: The Daily Star · Mar 21, 2022

Building Real-Life Skills

Students often graduate without essential skills like communication or teamwork. Our initiatives include quizzes, debates, and workshops to bridge that gap. We aim to make students both academically and socially competent.

Source: Kaler Kantho · Oct 5, 2022

Reducing Dropout Rates

Many students drop out due to lack of support, direction, and motivation. Shikkhar Alo organizes school outreach and counseling-based sessions. We work to keep students engaged and inspired throughout their journey.

Source: Jugantor · Dec 18, 2021

Recognizing Silent Achievers

Students from underprivileged backgrounds rarely get recognition. Our award ceremonies and receptions motivate students beyond grades. These events celebrate merit regardless of economic condition.

Source: Samakal · Apr 3, 2023

Lack of Extracurricular Platforms

Students have little access to creative outlets like debates and art contests. Shikkhar Alo fills this gap through inclusive competitions and fun learning. These help students grow beyond textbooks into well-rounded individuals.

Source: Ittefaq · Aug 12, 2022
