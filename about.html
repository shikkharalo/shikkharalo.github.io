About Us

Welcome to Shikkhar Alo

Shikkhar Alo is a youth-driven, voluntary and non-political organization committed to building a skilled future generation through its core philosophy: Initiative - Effort - Implementation. The organization relentlessly works to inspire students to focus on their education and overall intellectual growth. It conducts a range of activities including scholarships, quizzes, olympiads, art competitions, debate tournaments, felicitation of academic achievers and knowledge-sharing sessions. In todayâ€™s competitive world, surviving and thriving demands a blend of knowledge, skills, and timely adaptation. In a promising developing country like Bangladesh, merely relying on textbook education is not enough. Students must become critical thinkers, skillful, and confident..

Shikkhar Alo firmly believes that true development of a student lies not only in academic success, but also in personal growth, analytical thinking, problem-solving ability, and a sense of social responsibility. Whether from urban or rural areas, every student deserves equal opportunities. That is why we work at the grassroots level to bridge this gap. By actively involving parents, teachers, and the community, Shikkhar Alo strives to create a supportive educational environment. Our ultimate goal is to nurture a generation that is not only professionally competent but also morally upright and socially responsible citizens.
